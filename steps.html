Note: Click the Activity name to view the photo in its original resolution.

Activity 1: Computer Screen

Step 1: Distort the picture.

Click Transform → Distort

Activity 2: Out of Bounds

Step 1: Draw a rectangular marquee tool. It will be the border later on.

Step 2: Put a layer mask on the parts that are "out of bounds."

Step 3: Put a border by applying stroke on the rectangle marquee you made.

Step 4: Add a background and put it at the bottom.

Activity 3: Drawing Effect

Step 1: Duplicate the background layer. Put the copy on top of the original layer.

Step 2: Change the blending mode of the copy to "Color Dodge."

Step 3: Invert the image by holding CTRL + I.

Step 4: Use Gaussian Blur until the outlines of the image are visible.

Filter → Blur → Gaussian Blur

Step 5: Add an adjustment layer.

Step 6: Turn the saturation all the way down to -100.

Step 7: Add another adjustment layer.

Step 8: Adjust the black and white levels to your liking.

Activity 4: Fire Text Effect

Step 1: Color the background black.

Step 2: Write a text.

Step 3: Adjust the properties of the text to make it red.

Step 4: Get fire images.

Step 5: Put the fire images over the text.

Step 6: Adjust the properties of the fire images to make the text burning.

Activity 5: Tilt Shift Effect

Step 1: Blur the upper part of the photo.

Step 2: Blur the lower part.

Activity 6: Glowing Text Effect

Step 1: Color the background black.

Step 2: Write a text.

Step 3: Use the cloud filter the background.

Step 4: Color it green.

Activity 7: Starburst Effect

Step 1: Make the starburst effect using gradients and other filters.

Step 2: Turn it into a vector.

Activity 8: Photo to Sketch

Step 1: Filter → Stylize → Find Edges.

Step 2: Image → Adjustments → Desaturate.

Step 3: Put a layer mask.

Step 4: Add a black and white gradient.

Activity 9: Fiery Portrait

Step 1: Get fire images.

Step 2: Put it over the image.

Step 3: Adjust properties, opacity, and blending mode.
