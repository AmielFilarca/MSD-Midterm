My First Movie

For our first flash activity, we basically followed the instructions stated by our professor.
We made separate layers for the sky, trees, buildings, car, horn, stop action, and the
button. After drawing them, we added a sound provided my our professor.

Frame-by-Frame Animation

We were given instructions on how to make animations in different ways, this was the first
one. First, I created a plain text using the text tool. I inserted keyframes and move the letter
one by one. I repeated it several times to look like this.

Classic Tween

First, I made a ball and converted it into a symbol. Then I inserted a keyframe 50 frames
ahead and moved the ball to the right. Finally, I created a classic tween by right clicking on
the timeline and selecting 'Create Classic Tween'. I also made it rotate CW/CCW through
the properties panel.

Guided Classic Tween

I created a text with the text tool and then I broke it apart and distributed it to separate
layers. I added a guide layer by right clicking the topmost layer and selecting 'Add Classic
Motion Guide'. I made the animation path using the pencil tool. Then I created a classic
tween by selecting the object in the first frame and snapping its registration point to one
end of the path. And in the last frame, I snapped the object to the other end of the path. I
repeated this steps on all the layers.

Same steps as the one above but I didn't distribute the letters to each layer.

Shape Tween

I made a red circle on the first frame. Then I made a keyframe a few frames ahead and
made a rectangle. Then a star. I created a shape tween the same way I created classic
tween but instead of selecting 'Create Classic Tween' I chose 'Create Shape Tween'.
